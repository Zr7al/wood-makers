Our Story

Seven decades of
precision craft

Wood Maker Est. specializes in high-end custom woodwork and interior fit-out solutions, delivering premium craftsmanship across residential, commercial, and hospitality projects throughout Jordan and the wider region.

Originally founded in Palestine in 1952, the company carried its legacy through expansion to Kuwait and ultimately established its home in Amman, Jordan — where every piece continues to be crafted with the same founding commitment to excellence.

How We Work

The Atelier Method

01

Step 01

Consultation

Every project begins with a personal site visit. We listen, observe your space, and gather every detail of your vision — from functional needs to the finest aesthetic preferences — before a single line is drawn.

02

Step 02

Design & Planning

Our designers translate your brief into precise technical drawings. Every millimetre is measured, every material hand-selected. You review and approve the full plan before production begins.

03

Step 03

Production

Skilled craftsmen work in our dedicated workshop using premium-grade timber and the finest hardware. Every joint, every surface, every finish is subject to rigorous in-house quality control at each stage.

04

Step 04

Installation

Our installation team arrives prepared, protects your property, and works with surgical precision. Fittings are levelled, aligned, and secured — exactly as the drawings specify, with zero surprises.

05

Step 05

Handover

We walk you through every detail of the completed work, address any refinements on the spot, and provide care guidance. The project isn't closed until you're fully satisfied — and our support continues beyond.
