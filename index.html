Our Story

Seven decades of
precision craft

Wood Maker Est. specializes in high-end custom woodwork and interior fit-out solutions, delivering premium craftsmanship across residential, commercial, and hospitality projects throughout Jordan and the wider region.

Originally founded in Palestine in 1952, the company carried its legacy through expansion to Kuwait and ultimately established its home in Amman, Jordan — where every piece continues to be crafted with the same founding commitment to excellence.
