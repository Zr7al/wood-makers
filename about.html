Company Overview

Who We Are

Wood Maker Est. (Al-Jayousi Est.) was founded in 1952 with a singular purpose: to deliver woodwork of the highest caliber. What began as a craftsman's workshop in Palestine has grown into a fully integrated wood manufacturing and interior contracting company with a reputation that spans generations.

With decades of experience, the company has successfully delivered projects across hotels, hospitals, banks, government institutions, and private residences — each one a testament to the precision and care that define our work.

Today, operating from Amman, Jordan, we continue to serve clients who demand nothing less than exceptional — combining old-world craftsmanship with modern production capabilities.

"Our main focus is premium custom woodwork and professional fit-out finishing — delivered with the same dedication our founders instilled in 1952."

Our Journey

Seven Decades,
One Standard

Chapter One

1952

Founded in Palestine

Al-Jayousi Est. is established as a craftsman's workshop — a small team of master woodworkers setting a standard for quality that would define the company for generations to come.

Chapter Two

1970

Expansion to Kuwait

Growing regional demand drives a strategic expansion into Kuwait, where the company establishes itself as a trusted contractor for high-end commercial and hospitality interior fit-out projects.

Chapter Three

1985

Major Hospitality Contracts

The company completes landmark fit-out projects for several international hotels across the Gulf, cementing its reputation as the craftsman of choice for the high-end hospitality sector.

Chapter Four

1992

Relocation to Amman, Jordan

The company establishes its permanent headquarters in Amman — a strategic base for serving clients across the Levant and the wider Middle East, with deeper roots in the Jordanian market.

Chapter Five

2000s–

Continuous Growth

Expansion into banks, government institutions, private residences, and contemporary commercial spaces — all while maintaining the founding commitment to precision that has defined Wood Maker Est. since 1952.
