Company Overview

Who We Are

Wood Maker Est. (Al-Jayousi Est.) was founded in 1952 with a singular purpose: to deliver woodwork of the highest caliber. What began as a craftsman's workshop in Palestine has grown into a fully integrated wood manufacturing and interior contracting company with a reputation that spans generations.

With decades of experience, the company has successfully delivered projects across hotels, hospitals, banks, government institutions, and private residences — each one a testament to the precision and care that define our work.

Today, operating from Amman, Jordan, we continue to serve clients who demand nothing less than exceptional — combining old-world craftsmanship with modern production capabilities.

"Our main focus is premium custom woodwork and professional fit-out finishing — delivered with the same dedication our founders instilled in 1952."

Our Journey

Seven Decades,
One Standard

1952

Founded in Palestine

Al-Jayousi Est. is established as a woodworking workshop with a small team of master craftsmen, setting a standard for quality that would define the company for generations to come.

1970

Expansion to Kuwait

Growing regional demand leads to a strategic expansion into Kuwait, where the company establishes itself as a trusted contractor for commercial and hospitality interior fit-out projects.

1985

Major Hospitality Contracts

The company completes landmark fit-out projects for several international hotels in the Gulf, cementing its reputation in the high-end hospitality sector.

1992

Relocation to Amman, Jordan

The company establishes its permanent headquarters in Amman, Jordan — a strategic base for serving clients across the Levant and wider Middle East region.

2000s–Present

Continuous Growth

Expansion into banks, government institutions, private residences, and contemporary commercial spaces — all while maintaining the founding commitment to precision and quality that has defined Wood Maker Est. since 1952.
